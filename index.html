Get to know me!

I'm a Frontend Developer specializing in both mobile and web applications. With two years of hands-on experience, I’ve built Android apps from the ground up—designing local data storage with Room and SQLite, integrating Firebase for real-time syncing, and leveraging DataStore and Typesafe Navigation to deliver smooth, reliable user experiences.

Most recently, I’ve expanded into cross-platform development using Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP), allowing me to share logic between Android and other targets.

On the web side, I work daily with Next.js, crafting fast, SEO-friendly pages and seamless client-side navigation. For Android, I build with Jetpack Compose, embracing its declarative UI model, and I’m currently exploring Swift to broaden my native development toolkit.

I’m open to freelance opportunities of all kinds and excited to tackle new challenges that push both my skills and the product forward.
